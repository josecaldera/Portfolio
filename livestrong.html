Professional Work

Pursuing an Opportunity in the Web Fitness Space

At a Glance

Livestrong.com is a licensee of the Livestrong Organization's brand. We focus on content that helps readers get fit, eat better, manage weight, and overall - live well. As part of this project, our team reimagined what the fitness content web experience could be.

User Goal

Learn about the right ways to perform and execute individual exercises and comprehensive routines.

Business Goal

Position the Brand as a Leading Resource in Fitness Content

Role

Product Designer

Outcome

MVP was a success. The template became a core feature and gained more resource allocation.

Background

One autumn afternoon, members from engineering, community, business, and design met to chat and brainstorm on ways we could improve our core business KPIs:

  1. pageviews per session
  2. session duration times
  3. bounce rate
  4. rate of return users

The team came to the consensus that we should explore the idea of revamping the forum homepage, which was a directory of the subforums, to feature trending forum threads.

With that I was off to begin the design process.
