Professional Work

Fostering a Wider Conversation on FlyerTalk's Forums

At a Glance

FlyerTalk's forums are well-known in the frequent flyer and reward programs scene. As part of this project, we reworked our forum homepage to make it the central point for our communityâ€™s browsing behavior.

User Goal

Discover New Content and Sections of the Community

Business Goal

Improve Engagement and User Retention

Role

Associate Product Manager and UX Designer

Outcome

MVP was a success. The template became a core feature and gained more resource allocation.

Background

One autumn afternoon, members from engineering, community, business, and design met to chat and brainstorm on ways we could improve our core business KPIs:

  1. pageviews per session
  2. session duration times
  3. bounce rate
  4. rate of return users

The team came to the consensus that we should explore the idea of revamping the forum homepage, which was a directory of the subforums, to feature trending forum threads.

With that I was off to begin the design process.
