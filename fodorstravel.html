Professional Work

Redesigning Fodor's City Guides

At a Glance

Fodor's Travel is a travel guide that exists digitally and in print. As part of this project, we reworked our website's city template to surface the very best a destination has to offer, as well as, key information a traveler should know.

User Goal

Get a high level overview of a city to inform the planning process.

Business Goal

Improve Engagement and Increase Affiliate Conversion

Role

Product Manager and UX Designer

Outcome

MVP was a success. The template became a core feature and gained more resource allocation.

Background

Our company acquired Fodorâ€™s print and digital business in summer of 2016. We spent the rest of that year in migration mode and once that was complete, we began a top-down user experience and interface site redesign.

We spent the bulk of 2017 on homepage, editorial, and hotel review initiatives. As soon as 2018 rolled around, we began to focus on the destination guide section of the site. It was comprised of a hierarchy of the following templates: continent, country, state/region, city, neighborhoods, and so forth.

Project Frame

We focused on creating a rich page that surfaced the right amount of content from lower levels.
